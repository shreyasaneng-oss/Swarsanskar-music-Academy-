About Us

Swarsanskar Music Academy was founded by Shri Ranjit Gaonkar, a simple and humble man from the village of Ovliye in Sindhudurg.

His uncle, Namdevbuva Ghadigaonkar, was a well-known Adhyatmik Bhajan artist of his time, while his father was an Adhyatmik scholar. Coming from such a rich spiritual and musical background, music naturally became an important part of his life.

Ranjit Gaonkar began his music journey under Pt. Wamanrao Karane Guruji at Katta. Later, due to personal reasons, he shifted to Mumbai.
