Frequently Asked Questions

How to enroll for a course?

Visit the academy or call the contact number. After a short guidance session, a suitable batch will be allotted.

Do you prepare for Gandharva exams?

Yes, students are guided for Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya exams with proper syllabus and practice schedule.

Which age group can join?

Students from age 6 and above can join. Adult learners are also welcome in separate batches.

Online batches available?

Online or hybrid batches may be available depending on demand. Please contact the academy for current details.
